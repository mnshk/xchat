The project XCHAT is a digital platform which can be used to communicate with friends, team members or co-workers. This application can be used as a communication platform in organizations or businesses to establish an internal communication network with their peers.

Instead of using any social media platforms (like WhatsApp Groups or Telegram Groups) for communication. Companies, and other organisations (like schools, colleges, and businesses) can use this application, which can serve as a dedicated communication platform for professional use.

The XCHAT is simple, easy, and private.

In simple words, the XCHAT is an instant messaging platform which is inspired by the features of other social media platforms like WhatsApp (mostly), Instagram, Snapchat, and Telegram too.

The XCHAT is intended to improve the simplicity and privacy of the communication and deduct distractions out of the chatting experience. User can keep his/her personal profile or social media separate and use the XHCAT for professional communication.

You don't even have to share your phone number. The XCHAT works with your email address, and you must create a username which is unique for every user. Email address allows us to work globally, and you don't have to switch accounts if you move to other country (as in case of WhatsApp you must switch to another account as you change your phone number after moving to another country).

The XCHAT is platform and phone number independent.

The term XCHAT is pronounced as "cross chat" or "ex chat".

The "cross" or "ex" stands for the system platform independence or the application. The XCHAT is a Progressive Web Application. Being a web application, it can be used on Android devices, Windows NT platform, Apple's Mac system and iOS system too.
